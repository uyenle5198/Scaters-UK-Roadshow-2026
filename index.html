Tactical FAQ

Can I buy the Scaters Raptor 2026 series at the event?
Yes. The "The Predatory Hunt" edition of the Raptor 2026 decks is exclusive to the event and not available online.

What makes Raptor Roadshow 2026 different from previous years?
This is the largest campaign of 2026, themed "The Predatory Hunt". Unlike traditional contests, it transforms the streets of London, Manchester, and Glasgow into "Hunting Grounds", where skaters must conquer custom-designed street obstacles that mimic a natural predatory environment.

What does "The Predatory Hunt" mean in skateboarding context?
"The Predatory Hunt" refers to the skater's instinct to conquer. Each spot is considered "prey". Participants must use skill and creativity to successfully "hunt" (land tricks) on iconic urban architecture.
